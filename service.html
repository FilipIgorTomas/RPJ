Service Processes and Technology

To ensure the highest quality, the following steps are performed for each service:

1. Base Grinding

Removes old wax and prepares the base for new layers.

2. Final Grinding

Provides a smooth surface to optimize glide.

3. Edge Tuning

Adjusts the angle for better control and performance.

4. Edge Polishing

Smoothens the edges to avoid resistance on the snow.

5. Wax Application

Applies high-quality wax for protection and speed.

6. Race Edge

Delivers sharp, precise edges for professional use.
